Harris Ranch, CA

24553 W Dorris Ave, Coalinga, CA

Harris Ranch has been around since 1937. It is the perfect destination to stop and get some gas and enjoy a delicious BBQ meal. They are a 24 hour operation with people arriving as early as 5am to hit the smokers up. Try the brisket or trip tip and enjoy their delicious sides.
